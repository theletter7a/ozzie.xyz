Proteins

Monomer: Amino Acids

Example: Insulin

Function: They can be enzymes that help process and carry out necessary functions in the cell.

Carbohydates

Monomer: Monosaccharides

Example: Glucose

Function: store short term and easy access energy

Nucleic Acids

Monomer: Nucleotides

Example: DNA

Function: Code for the production of proteins and carry genetic information

Lipids

Monomer: None

Example: Wax

Function: They're hydrophobic and hold long term energy
